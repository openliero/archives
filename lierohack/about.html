About

Liero is a very customizable game. Almost everything can be changed: Levels, graphics, sounds weapons, fonts, palettes, CPU settings. My goal is now to make a complete reference to all that can be changed. Big parts of the offset list came from 'research' done by me and Johan Otterud. Because Johan Otterud didn't reply to my e-mails for a long time, I decided to upload the list, so everyone could use it. If you have any additions or corrections, please send them to me and I will upload it. Document format descriptions are also welcome. Anyone sending in useful information will be listed in the credits section.

I know there are a lot of Liero fans who have the skills to make a liero editor, but don't know enough about Liero itself. Now they get a chance too. If you use any information from this website, please include credits to the author(s) of the documents used in your program.

- Tim Verweij
